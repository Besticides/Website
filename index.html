About Besticides Minerals Private Limited

Company Profile & Our Story

Besticides Minerals Private Limited is a pioneering company dedicated to sustainable agriculture through the manufacture and distribution of organic fertilizers, bio-stimulants, and bio-fertilizers. Founded in 2014, the company is committed to advancing chemical-free farming practices that promote soil, plant, and environmental health across India.

Our journey began with Mr. Duggineni Bhaskara Rao, an experienced Geophysics Scientist with two decades of expertise at the Oil & Natural Gas Corporation. His scientific background and commitment to sustainability inspired the company's establishment, with a vision to revolutionize agriculture in India through organic farming. Under his leadership as Managing Director, the company has grown steadily, driven by a focus on innovation, quality, and farmer-centric solutions.

Our Mission

To educate, inspire, and empower farmers to transition towards organic agriculture by making it cost-effective, productive, and sustainable. We aim to establish best-in-class manufacturing practices for bio-fertilizers, bio-stimulants, and organic chelated minerals, designed to meet the real-time needs of Indian farmers while reducing cultivation costs. Through this dual focus on vision and mission, Besticides Minerals seeks to foster a systemic shift towards greener agricultural practices that benefit farmers, consumers, and the environment alike.

Leadership and Team Profile

Besticides Minerals Private Limited was founded by Mr. Duggineni Bhaskara Rao, an experienced Geophysics Scientist with two decades of expertise at the Oil & Natural Gas Corporation. His scientific background and commitment to sustainability inspired the company's establishment in 2014.

Under his leadership as Managing Director, the company has grown steadily, driven by a focus on innovation, quality, and farmer-centric solutions. The leadership team brings together expertise in scientific research, sustainable agriculture, manufacturing excellence, and market development to ensure Besticides Minerals remains at the forefront of the organic input sector in India.

Infrastructure & Operational Reach

Our corporate headquarters is strategically located in Ameerpet, Hyderabad, providing central access to metropolitan markets, research institutions, and customers. The manufacturing facility is situated in Nandigama, Andhra Pradesh, equipped with state-of-the-art machinery and adhering to stringent quality management and environmental standards.

Currently, Besticides Minerals operates across inter-state markets in India with plans to expand nationally and eventually enter international markets. This expansion is supported by scalable infrastructure, skilled manpower, and logistics networks designed to maintain supply chain efficiency and customer responsiveness.

Financial Performance Overview

Besticides Minerals has experienced strong financial growth over recent fiscal years, reflecting market acceptance and gradual expansion:

  • FY 2023-24 revenue: Rs. 1.8 Crores
  • FY 2023-24 provisional revenue: Rs. 2.2 Crores
  • Current credit facility liability (as of 31/03/2025): Rs. 3 Crores
  • Factory asset valuation: Rs. 22 Crores

This financial trajectory highlights increasing demand for organic farming inputs and effective company management aimed at sustainable profitability. The company is well-positioned to leverage growth opportunities in India's expanding organic sector.

Impact & Sustainability Commitment

Sustainability lies at the heart of Besticides Minerals' business philosophy. Our products and practices are designed specifically to lower environmental impact by reducing dependence on synthetic chemicals, thereby lowering CO2 emissions and improving soil health. We actively promote organic farming methods that preserve biodiversity and water quality, contributing to resilient agro-ecosystems. Through farmer education, community involvement, and product innovation, we empower agricultural communities to embrace sustainable practices that ensure long-term food security and ecosystem balance.

Future Outlook & Strategic Recommendations

Besticides Minerals Private Limited is primed for growth driven by increasing awareness and demand for sustainable agriculture solutions in India and beyond. Strategic priorities to support this growth include:

  • Expanding agricultural support nationwide through branches, factories, and a large corporate R&D center.
  • Expanding product lines with cutting-edge bio-fertilizers and stimulants tailored for diverse Indian agro-climatic zones.
  • Investing in advanced R&D to enhance product efficacy and develop cost-effective formulations.
  • Strengthening farmer outreach programs to accelerate adoption of organic farming practices.
  • Scaling operational infrastructure and logistics to serve national and export markets efficiently.
  • Building partnerships with government and non-government organizations to promote sustainable agriculture policies.

By executing these strategies with steadfast commitment to quality, innovation, and community impact, Besticides Minerals aims to become a benchmark in India’s organic agriculture revolution, delivering value to investors, farmers, and the environment alike.

Our Innovative Products & Services

Besticides Minerals specializes in manufacturing and processing a diverse range of organic agricultural inputs designed to improve soil fertility, enhance plant growth, and protect crops naturally. Our products cater to individual farmers managing small holdings as well as large-scale corporate farms, providing scalable and effective solutions that improve yield quality and sustainability.

Bio-Fertilizers & Bio-Stimulants

Premium Organic Fertilizers

Designed to improve soil fertility and enrich soil nutrients naturally without chemical residues, promoting healthier and more productive crops.

Advanced Bio-Stimulants

Enhance plant growth, nutrient absorption, and resilience against environmental stresses, leading to stronger, more vibrant plants.

Microbial Bio-Fertilizers

Containing beneficial microorganisms to significantly improve soil microbial activity and overall soil health, fostering a thriving ecosystem.

Chelated Minerals & Allied Products

Chelated Minerals for Growth

Specialized chelated minerals designed to improve plant growth and significantly boost crop yields by ensuring optimal nutrient availability.

Bio-Pesticides & Fungicides

Effective, organic solutions to protect crops from pests and fungal diseases, promoting plant health and resilience naturally without harmful chemicals.

Essential Amino Acids

Allied products like Amino Acids to support crop metabolism and vitality, ensuring plants have the building blocks for optimal growth and stress recovery.
