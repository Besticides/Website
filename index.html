Growing a Sustainable Future

Besticides Minerals Private Limited is a pioneering company dedicated to sustainable agriculture through the manufacture and distribution of organic fertilizers, bio-stimulants, and bio-fertilizers. Founded in 2014, the company is committed to advancing chemical-free farming practices that promote soil, plant, and environmental health across India.

Our Vision

At Besticides Minerals, our mission is to make ethical & planet-friendly organic food available to everyone. We dedicate ourselves to fostering a global shift towards sustainable organic fertilizers, bio fertilizers, bio stimulants, bio pesticides, organic acids, and chelated minerals. Our mission extends beyond availability — educating and empowering communities about organic benefits for a healthier future.

Core Values Driving Our Success

  • Sustainability: Prioritizing environmental stewardship to protect resources.
  • Innovation: Continuous R&D investment for next-gen agriculture solutions.
  • Integrity: Transparency, honesty, and ethics in all dealings.
  • Customer Focus: Enhancing farmer yield and livelihoods.
  • Excellence in Manufacturing: High-quality, effective inputs.
  • Community Commitment: Awareness and education in rural regions.
  • Affordability & Value Creation: Accessible pricing, maximum ROI.

About Besticides Minerals Private Limited

Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2014 by Mr. Duggineni Bhaskara Rao, a seasoned Geophysics Scientist, Besticides Minerals Private Limited aims to revolutionize agriculture by educating and empowering farmers across India. We provide high-quality, cost-effective organic solutions to foster greener farming practices benefiting farmers, consumers, and the environment alike.

Manufacturing, Production & Distribution

Our manufacturing facility in Nandigama, Andhra Pradesh, equipped with advanced machinery, produces 500 tons/month of organic fertilizers and pesticides to meet growing demand effectively.

We've built a strong dealership network across major agricultural states in India, ensuring product availability and timely delivery to both urban and remote farmers.

Research & Development and Quality Control

Innovation and quality define us. Our in-house R&D lab conducts continuous research, development, and testing to create effective products tailored for Indian soil and crops.

Strict quality control upon every batch ensures efficacy, purity, safety, and trust from our farmers.

Product1

Product2

A specialized bio-fertilizer containing a rich blend of beneficial microorganisms. Its primary function is to improve soil microbial activity and overall health, making the soil more fertile and productive for long-term use.

Product3
