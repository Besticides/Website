Growing a Sustainable Future

Besticides Minerals Private Limited is a pioneering company dedicated to sustainable agriculture through the manufacture and distribution of organic fertilizers, bio-stimulants, and bio-fertilizers. Founded in 2014, the company is committed to advancing chemical-free farming practices that promote soil, plant, and environmental health across India.

Our Vision

At Besticides Minerals, our Mission is to make ethical & Planet friendly organic food available to everyone. At Besticides, we are dedicated to fostering a global shift towards sustainable organic fertilizers, Bio fertilizers, Bio stimulants, Bio pesticides, Organic acids, chelated minerals choices. Our mission extends beyond mere availability, aiming to educate and empower communities about the benefits of choosing organic options. By prioritizing environmental responsibility, we strive to create a healthier and more sustainable future for all.

About Besticides Minerals Private Limited

Our Story & Mission

Besticides Minerals Private Limited, founded in 2014 by Mr. Duggineni Bhaskara Rao, a seasoned Geophysics Scientist, is dedicated to revolutionizing agriculture. Our mission is to educate, inspire, and empower farmers across India to transition to organic, sustainable farming by providing high-quality, cost-effective solutions. We aim to foster a systemic shift toward greener practices that benefit farmers, consumers, and the environment alike.

Infrastructure, Production & Distribution

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Nandigama, Andhra Pradesh, is equipped with advanced machinery to produce high-quality organic fertilizers, bio-stimulants, and pesticides. Our current capacity is approximately 500 tons per month, allowing us to meet the growing demand for our products efficiently.

We've established an extensive dealership network spanning key agricultural states in India. This robust distribution channel ensures our products are accessible to farmers in both urban and remote areas, supported by a dedicated logistics team that guarantees timely delivery and customer responsiveness.

Research & Development and Quality Control

At Besticides Minerals, innovation and quality are at the core of what we do. Our in-house R&D lab is dedicated to continuous research, development, and testing of new formulations. We work to create innovative and effective products that are specifically tailored to the needs of Indian soil and crops.

We adhere to stringent quality control measures at every stage of production, from raw material sourcing to the final product. Our lab meticulously tests each batch to ensure it meets our high standards for efficacy, purity, and safety. This commitment to quality is what earns the trust of our farmers.
